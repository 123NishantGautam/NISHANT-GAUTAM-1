INTRODUCTION TO AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that focuses on creating machines or systems capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, decision-making, and understanding natural language. AI is used in many areas such as healthcare, education, business, transportation, and security to make processes faster, smarter, and more efficient. From virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa to self-driving cars and advanced medical diagnosis systems, AI is transforming the way we live and work, making it one of the most impactful technologies of the modern era.

What is AI

Ai is a machin

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and make decisions like humans. It enables computers and systems to perform tasks such as recognizing speech, understanding languages, solving problems, analyzing data, and adapting to new situations. Unlike traditional software that follows fixed instructions, AI can improve its performance over time using techniques like machine learning. In simple terms, AI allows machines to act smart and perform complex activities that usually need human intelligence.
